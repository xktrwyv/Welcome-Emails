About Welcome.US

Welcome.US is a national initiative built to mobilize Americans from all corners of the country to welcome and support those seeking refuge here.

Americans in 12,000 zip codes and across all 50 states have stepped forward to welcome newcomers through legal pathways.

Learn more.

Our Story

Born out of the urgent response to the fall of Kabul in 2021 and strengthened as Americans welcomed Ukrainians fleeing war, our work has grown from an emergency effort to help refugee newcomers into a movement that reflects Americans’ desire to welcome through sustainable solutions that bridge divides.
