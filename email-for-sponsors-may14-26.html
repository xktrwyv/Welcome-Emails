Become a Citizen Guide

As a Citizen Guide, you are matched one-on-one with a green card holder as they prepare for the U.S. citizenship exam. This program is entirely virtual, with a flexible schedule. You will serve as a citizenship test prep tutor, helping a green card holder prepare for their citizenship exam by practicing English reading and writing and studying U.S. civics questions. We provide self-paced training, ready-made study materials, and support for you throughout the process.

Citizen Guides aren't experts or experienced tutors. They're Welcomers, coaches, mentors, and friends, which is exactly what you've been.

So far, our volunteers are finding this program to be deeply rewarding and flexible enough to fit into their daily lives. As we prepare for our full launch this summer, we’re looking for a few more Welcomers to help us get it right.
