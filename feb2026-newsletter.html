STORIES OF WELCOME

A bridge of compassion from Ukraine

After experiencing the compassion and hospitality of the Ukrainian people during his service in the Peace Corps, Corey Retell knew he had to act when Russia invaded years later. Through the Uniting for Ukraine (U4U) program, he sponsored a friend and welcomed him to his Texas community. His dedication to the people of Ukraine and fostering a welcoming community at home earned Corey the Heart of a Welcomer recognition.

Read his story →

Supporting Ukrainians on a path forward

What began in 2022 as a rapid-response coalition of legal volunteers has grown into a large-scale educational nonprofit and a leading advocate for Ukrainian newcomers in the United States. Composed of nearly 250 attorneys and resettlement experts, the Ukraine Immigration Task Force (UITF) serves as a critical bridge between complex, ever-changing U.S. immigration policy and the people it impacts.
